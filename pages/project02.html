SUMMARY
The design is focused in the positioning and design of an experimental contemporary villa in the dramatic landscape of a limestone quarry: an ensemble of a house and garden. The ensemple is sited stratigically and engages with the particular qualities of the landscape while the design is used as a basis for the investigation of the hidden qualities of the landscape.
